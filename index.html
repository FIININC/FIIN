MoneyMate is a first of its kind micro-lending service that builds a savings deposit for the borrower. It gives consumers fast and convenient access to a small-dollar loan in times of need, and simultaneously helps them create a savings buffer in case they fall short again on any upcoming bills and obligations.

The mission of MoneyMate is to end the debt-trap cycle that affects millions of people around the world due to the predatory practices of the Payday Industry. Borrowers of these loans are financially vulnerable consumers that lack access to healthy empowering loans and MoneyMate is on a quest to change that!
